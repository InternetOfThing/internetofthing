INTERNET*OF*THINGS

The world around US

Bluetooth wireless technology with low power consumption (Bluetooth low energy, Bluetooth LE, presented as Bluetooth Smart) breathes life into mikromayachki on batteries, who live their lives for years, exchange with each other data about the world and transmit signals to smartphones, tablets, laptops, watches, bracelets, or any devices.

People drink coffee, walk, stand in line for a cupcake and get interesting links or phone numbers from the outside world, in the form of advertisements, alerts, warnings on their smartphones or tablets with support for Bluetooth 4. Apple company has established a kind of beacon IBEACON, Google has created a type of company beacon EddyStone, other companies have their own types. Beacons are distinguished only by the server, which is programmed within them. All BLE Bluetooth Beacons typically operate within 7-15 meters, can work up to 2 years on a conventional flat battery, and cost about $ 8. People can learn about the place gle they are watching video via the link beacon, call that Beacon offers, find out about discounts for the goods, how to pass along the corridor in an airport terminal, download the link beacon documents to fill in the registry , meet with someone who put his beacon with information about themselves. Beacons can shoot on the humidity data, lighting, temperature and other parameters of the world, pass this information to other beacons. BlueTooth Beacons can also transmit TX \ RX data via the serial port work as an electromechanical relay and open the door, enable or disable the blinds, all appliances, and more.

iBeacon Settings



HOW TO

1) Connect to beacons
2) Give the command to beacon

change iBeacon`s tittle

0) Create a name in the application "Browser of Things"
1) Turn the power on Beacon
2) Install any Bluetooth terminal application to communicate with the beacon, such as "BlueTooth Terminal"
3) Add a beacon in the Bluetooth devices - the default password 000000 (or 1234)
4) Connect to the beacon
5) Give the command to change the name in the Beacon`s application
a. AT+NAMBName
or
b. AT+NAMEName
Depending on the model of iBeacon
6) Make reboot of iBeacon
a. Power off ibeacon
or
b. AT+RESET и poweroff BlueTooth in the Android settings

change UUID of iBeacon

0) Start the application ID in "Browser of Things"
1) Turn the power on Beacon
2) Install any Bluetooth terminal application to communicate with the beacon, such as "BlueTooth Terminal"
3) Add a beacon in the Bluetooth devices - the default password 000000 (or 1234)
4) Connect to the beacon
5) Give 4 commands to change the UUID
(UUID consists of 4 parts)
a. AT+IBE0XXXXXXXX
b. AT+IBE1XXXXXXXX
c. AT+IBE2XXXXXXXX
d. AT+IBE3XXXXXXXX
6) Make restart beacon
a. Restart iBeacon power
or
b. AT+RESET и poweroff BlueTooth в Android

Connecto to PC

1) Start the «Assistant» program to communicate with the beacon
2) Insert the iBeacon into USB of PC
3) Choose COM-port of Ibeacon
4) Give commands to ibeacons

Firmware Update

0) Connect iBeacon only through a PC to the USB or UART-USB
1) Close all programs to work with COM-port beacon
2) Run the program from the archive with HMsoft.exe firmware
3) Select only the firmware for your model beacon
4) chosen COM-port in the program HMsoft
